Vim seems to be like Marmite - you love it or you hate it. Here is my attempt at making it a little less odious:

`x` - delete  
`A` - append to line  
`a` - append to word  
`dw` - delete word  
`d3w` - delete 3 words  
`de` - delete word (leaves space)  
`0` - start of line
